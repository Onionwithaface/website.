About Us

Welcome to the Plant Immunology Laboratory. Our mission is to advance understanding of plant immune mechanisms and contribute to sustainable agriculture. We value our people and prioritize humanity alongside scientific excellence.

Research

Our research focuses on:

  • Identification of virulence and avirulence functions in HopZ effectors
  • Genome-wide association studies on microbial pathogenicity
  • Mechanisms of plant-pathogen interactions
